Your Career Path Begins Here

The primary objective of the program is to create awareness and promote technical skills among women. Through various outreach programs, the initiative educates women on the benefits of acquiring technical skills, dispelling the notion that such fields are only meant for men. The program aims to change the perception of technical courses as a male-dominated area and encourage more women to pursue technical courses.

The program also aims to encourage women to pursue career opportunities in fields such as IT, Computer and Phone Repair , and electronics repair.
